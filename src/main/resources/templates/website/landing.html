Scan Websites and Files

ThreatAlert uses the power of the VirusTotal API to inform users of potential malware, phishing attempts, or other security threats that could harm users. It scans a URL or file and provides a comprehensive report of potential threats to users.

Overview and Summary

Through the use of the OpenAI API, ThreatAlert utlises the power of Artificial Intelligence to generate a short summary of any detected threats of scanned items. ThreatAlert aids users in swiftly understanding any potential threats and how to proceed with these threats in mind.

Community-Based Feedback

ThreatAlert fosters a community of security-conscious users who can share their experiences browsing various websites. Users can leave comments on scanned websites, as well as view experiences of other users through their comments.
